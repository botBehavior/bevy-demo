▶ PLAY:
  cargo run --release

🎯 CONTROLS:
  Mouse       - Move/aim
  Click/Space - Trail weapon (deadly follow trail)
  Tab         - Wave weapon (curved projectiles)
  Esc         - Pause/unpause

💎 POWER-UPS:
  🔴 Circle     - Heart (+1 health)
  🔵 Tall Rect  - Shield (4s invincibility)
  🟡 Square     - Damage (+50% per stack)
  🟣 Circle     - Accuracy (snappier movement)

🎮 TIPS:
  • Keep moving - survival is mobility
  • Build combos for 5x score multiplier
  • Stack damage upgrades for power fantasy
  • Shield is tactical (only 4s!)
  • Accuracy makes movement more responsive
